Phishing Awareness Training

What is Phishing?

Phishing is a cyber attack where attackers pretend to be trusted sources to steal sensitive information like passwords and credit card details.

How to Recognize Phishing Emails

  • Suspicious sender address
  • Urgent or threatening language
  • Unexpected attachments
  • Strange links

Fake Websites

Always check URLs carefully. Fake websites often mimic real ones but have slight spelling differences.
